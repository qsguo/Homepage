This is an introduction course to computer science and is offered to teach you Think as Computer Scientists. We will discuss a number of concepts pertaining to computer science. The course includes lectures and quizzes, final exams, optional additional exercises with solutions, and other readings and resources. In addition, there will be one project to help you to get familiar with computer science.
CS101 is aimed at students with little or no programming experience. It is to provide students with an understanding of the role computation can play in solving problems. It also aims to help students, regardless of their major, to feel justifiably confident of their ability to write small programs that allow them to accomplish useful goals.
