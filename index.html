I am a PhD student in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science (IMADA) at SDU. Before joining SDU, I was studying at Information School of Renmin University for my master degree. Now, I am working on my doctral degree under supervising of Prof. Yongluan Zhou.

Research Interests

My research interests range across database, cloud computing, and big data analysis. My current focus is on multi-paradigm analytics of big data. I am now working on two topics: (1) optimizing the resources utilization for large-scale distributed stream processing; and (2) applying the piecewise linear models to enhance the approximate query processing (AQP) in interactive analysis of big data.
