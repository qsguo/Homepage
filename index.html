About Me

I am now a stuff member of School of Computer Science and Control Engineering, North University of China (NUC). Before joining NUC, I was working on my doctoral thesis under supervising of Prof. Yongluan Zhou in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science (IMADA), University of Southern Denmark. Before then, I had obtained my master degree at Renmin University.

    • Research

    My research interests range across a variaty of field, such as database, data mining, cloud computing, and big data analysis. They now can be subsumed by the a intersecting discipline entitiled "Data Science".

    Current Projects

    My current focus is on multi-paradigm analytics of big data. I am now working on the following two projects.

    (1) Optimization of Large-scale Stream Processing
    optimizing the resources utilization for large-scale distributed stream processing.
    (2) Approximate Query Processing (AQP)
    applying the piecewise linear models to enhance the approximate query processing (AQP) in interactive analysis of big data.
